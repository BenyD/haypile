# Kitchen renovation planning

## Budget

Total budget: $18,400. Contingency 15% on top ($2,760) because every
renovation forum says overruns are guaranteed.

## Quotes

- Hartwell Construction: $16,900, eight weeks, can start in June
- Delgado & Sons: $19,750, five weeks, start immediately
- (Third quote pending from Novak Interiors)

## Decisions

Going with white oak cabinets over painted MDF — costs about $2,100 more but
the samples look dramatically better and won't chip. Quartz counters over
marble: marble stains, we cook with turmeric weekly.

## Open questions

Whether moving the sink to the island is worth the ~$3,000 plumbing work.
Contractor says the drain slope is the hard part, not the supply lines.
